Gatwick METAR WhatsApp Service

How to Use

Send a WhatsApp message containing "metar", "weather", or "gatwick" to our number to receive the latest METAR information for Gatwick Airport.

What is METAR?

METAR is a format for reporting weather information used by pilots and air traffic control. It provides essential weather data for aviation.

Service Status

The WhatsApp METAR service is currently active and ready to receive messages.
